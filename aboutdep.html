ABOUT US



Welcome!

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at UG, where innovation and excellence converge to shape the future of technology. With a legacy steeped in pioneering research and academic distinction, our department stands as a beacon of cutting-edge discovery and scholarly achievement. At the heart of our institution lies a relentless pursuit of knowledge, driven by a diverse community of scholars, researchers, and visionaries. Our commitment to pushing the boundaries of computer science permeates every facet of our curriculum, research endeavors, and collaborative initiatives.
Academic Rigor and Innovation: Encompassing a rigorous academic framework, our programs blend theoretical foundations with practical application. Our faculty comprises esteemed leaders and industry experts, fostering an environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills essential for addressing the complexities of the digital era.
Research Excellence: Pioneering research serves as the cornerstone of our department. Our faculty and students engage in groundbreaking investigations across a spectrum of disciplines within computer science, spanning artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, human-computer interaction, and beyond. Through interdisciplinary collaborations and access to state-of-the-art facilities, we drive forward the frontiers of technological innovation.
Empowering Global Impact: We believe in leveraging technology for the betterment of society. Our department encourages an ethos of social responsibility, encouraging students and faculty to apply their expertise to solve real-world challenges. From advancing healthcare technologies to shaping sustainable computing solutions, our community is dedicated to making a meaningful global impact.
Inclusive Community and Diversity: Embracing diversity in all its forms, we foster an inclusive environment where individuals from varied backgrounds collaborate, learn, and thrive. We celebrate the richness that diverse perspectives bring to the field of computer science, encouraging an open exchange of ideas and experiences.
Preparing Future Leaders: Our mission extends beyond academic excellence; we are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders in technology. Equipping our graduates with the skills, knowledge, and ethical compass to navigate an evolving landscape, we empower them to become trailblazers in their respective fields.
Join us on a transformative journey at the forefront of technological innovation. Together, we shape the future, one algorithm, one breakthrough, and one visionary idea at a time.
The range of courses available in the department and the skills acquired by students enable them to find employment in many diverse fields of the economy and prepare our graduates for the computing industry, working in a project team (or research and development team), and to enable the graduate to bring specialist skills to that team. The course also provides the platform for further study or research through exposure to established and emerging technologies and methods, and to active fields of research in Computer Science. The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
